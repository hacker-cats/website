# About Hackercats

We are a passionate group of student programmers and hackers at Montana State University. Our club brings together students who are interests in cybersecurity topics.

## Our Mission

- **Educate** - Cultivate a community of students that are passionate about Cybersecurity
- **Learn** - Educate students and members of the public on cybersecurity topics and career opportunities.
- **Compete** - Participate and host capture-the-flag (CTF) events where we can build and test our skills

## What We Do

- **CTF Competitions**: We actively participate in a few Capture-The-Flag cybersecurity competitions each semester.
- **Mentoring**: Experienced members guide newcomers through their cybersecurity journey. We provide a set of lectures and hands-on work to learn about hacking.
- **Community Events**: We organize CTF events for local community organizations like the Bozeman High School and other cybersecurity curious groups.

## Club Values

- **Inclusive**: Everyone is welcome, regardless of skill level
- **Collaborative**: We learn better together
- **Innovative**: Always exploring new technologies and strategies
- **Community-focused**: Building projects that benefit our larger community, our school, and ultimately ourselves in the process.
